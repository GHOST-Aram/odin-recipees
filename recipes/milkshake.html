Introduction

Reminiscent of countertop service and carhop days, there is nothing better than a perfect milkshake.

Ingriedients

FOR THE ULTUMATE VANILLA MILKSHAKE

  • 4 large scoops(about 1.5 c.) vanilla ice cream
  • Whipped topping, for garnish
  • Sprinkles for garnish
  • Maraschino cherry, for garnish

Directions

Step 1

In a blender, blend together ice cream and Milk

Step 2

Pour into a glass and garnish with whipped topping, Sprinkles and cherry.
